Hi there. This is Fawn Nguyen. I started VisualPatterns.org in late 2013, and a lot of cool people have contributed to make the site what it is.

The site now has 500 patterns, so I'm no longer accepting new submissions. I do have the answer key with all the equations — if you'd like it (and to not undermine your students' work), please email me from your school account.

Please reach out with any questions or comments.

How to Use Visual Patterns

This 6-lesson workshop (around 60 minutes total of video lessons) is completely self-paced.

Price: $49

In the lessons, I share with you everything I know and love about using visual patterns with my students. It really is a game-changer, when implemented successfully, in helping students sharpen their algebraic reasoning and critical thinking.

  • Lesson 1: How do we use visual patterns?
  • Lesson 2: How do we use quadratic patterns?
  • Lesson 3: What instructional moves should we hold off on?
  • Lesson 4: How do we help students see patterns with triangular numbers and patterns beyond quadratic?
  • Lesson 5: How else may we use visual patterns?
  • Lesson 6: How do we facilitate visual patterns in distance learning?
  • Lesson 7 (optional): Teachers' testimonies on using visual patterns with their students

I hope you'll consider signing up for the workshop and/or pass it along to a colleague who may be interested. Thank you so much.
